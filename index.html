About

Hi, I'm Anjishnu, I work as an applied machine learning scientist on Alexa. I current work in the Cambridge office and focus on open domain question answering.

I enjoy building cutting edge consumer facing products that are made possible by machine learning. On the research side, my interest lies mainly in the intersection of representation learning and zero-shot learning and knowledge representation. The deep learning algorithms of today are extremely resource hungry, in terms of data, computation and size - whereas humans are able to perform exceptionally well at a task with only a single representative example.

Prior to Cambridge, I worked in Seattle where I had the experience of building one of the biggest machine learning powered platforms of today, The Alexa Skills Kit (ASK) This project was an incredible learning experience in terms of building machine learning systems with resource constraints and in terms of ML platform design.
